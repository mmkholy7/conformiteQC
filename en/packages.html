Frequently Asked Questions

What is a privacy officer?

+

A privacy officer is a person designated by your organization to assume all legal responsibilities related to personal data protection under Loi 25. This person must be identified with the Commission d'accès à l'information du Québec.

Can I change packages at any time?

+

Yes, you can modify your package at any time according to the evolution of your needs. We offer complete flexibility with monthly or quarterly contracts according to your preference.

What happens in case of a data breach?

+

Our Growth and Premium packages include complete incident management. We handle all legal notifications, communication with authorities and affected individuals, as well as post-incident follow-up.

Are there setup fees?

+

No, there are no setup fees. The monthly price includes everything: official designation, policy implementation, initial training and all services included in your package.

How does employee training work?

+

We offer training adapted to your industry sector, available online or in-person. Training includes comprehension tests and participation certificates for your compliance files.

Can I get a custom quote?

+

Absolutely! Contact us for a free consultation and we'll develop a custom package according to your specific needs, industry sector and organization size.
